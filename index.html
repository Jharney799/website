Who's this James guy?

I'm a student of technology, a philosopher, an ever aspiring stoic, a husband, friend, hacker, and creative. I'm a fully realized creation and the author of this website. Welcome and thanks for visiting my corner of the internet.

Alright, what's this place?

A self-hosted website written and maintained by me, James Harney. Originally I foresaw a spot where I could slowly collect code I have written and post my blog, of course that's lame and I couldn't be bothered.

Instead of some soulless repository for various things, I am planning on making this place a personal source of knowledge with all my favorite stuff around the internet. I have drawn great inspirations from Derek Sivers and his excellent guide on self-hosting and writing your own website and recommend everyone reads the following before they spend another minute online.
